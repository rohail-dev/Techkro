About Tech Kro - Home Study Module 2020

What is home study module?

It is a 8-day program that teaches you very basic programming to help you develop the right skills for the Tech Karo program. You will be given videos to watch and after each video you will have to take a short quiz.
At the end of the 8-day program, you will have to submit a short technical project. If you successfully complete the 10-day home study module, you will no longer have to give Math, English, and Computer test as part
of the interview process. You will only have to give a short interview after which your admission to the course is guaranteed.
